Profile

Why quality is my craft

I'm a Systems Engineer and Data Analyst based in Bogotá, Colombia. My career has been built on a single principle: accuracy matters. Whether validating high-volume datasets or testing web applications, my job has always been to find what's wrong before it reaches the end user.

At Concentrix, I spent years ensuring the integrity of operational data — spotting inconsistencies, documenting issues, and collaborating with technical teams to fix root causes. Earlier, as a web development specialist, I used JIRA daily to track bugs and tested applications before delivery.

I bring a rare combination to QA: the analytical discipline of a data professional, the technical understanding of a developer, and a genuinely methodical mindset that thrives on detail-heavy, repetitive work others find tedious.
